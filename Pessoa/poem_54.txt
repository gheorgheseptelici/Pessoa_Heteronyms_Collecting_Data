I daydream, far from my cozy Self-awareness as a man. I don’t know who my soul is, Nor does it know who I am. Understand it? It would take time. Explain it? Don’t know if I can. And in this misunderstanding Between who I am and what is I There’s a whole other meaning Lying between earth and sky. In that gap is born the universe With suns and stars past counting. It has a profound meaning, Which I know. It’s outside me.
