Rolling wave that returns, Smaller, to the sea that brought you, Scattering as you retreat, As if the sea were nothing— Why, on your return journey, Do you take only your disappearing? Why don’t you also take My heart to that ancient sea? I’ve had it for such a long time That I’m tired of having to feel it. So take it in that faint murmur By which I hear you ﬂeeing!
