XLV A row of trees in the distance, toward the slope... But what is a row of trees? There are just trees. “Row” and the plural “trees” are names, not things. Unhappy human beings, who put everything in order, Draw lines from thing to thing, Place labels with names on absolutely real trees, And plot parallels of latitude and longitude On the innocent earth itself, which is so much greener and full of ﬂowers!
